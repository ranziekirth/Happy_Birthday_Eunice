Put your photos in this folder.

1. MAIN PHOTO (shown inside the book, then centered on the final screen)
   - Name it: main-photo.jpg
   - (Any image works, just keep the name, or update MAIN_IMAGE in script.js
      if you'd rather use a different filename.)

2. SCATTERED / RANDOM PHOTOS (the extra photos scattered around the final screen)
   - Name them anything you like, e.g. photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, beach-trip.png ...
   - Then list each filename inside script.js, in the GALLERY_IMAGES array
     near the top of the file:

       const GALLERY_IMAGES = [
         "assets/images/photo1.jpg",
         "assets/images/photo2.jpg",
         "assets/images/beach-trip.png",
       ];

   - Add as many lines as you want — every line becomes one scattered photo.

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp, .gif

Delete this text file once you've added your real photos (optional, it has no effect on the site).
